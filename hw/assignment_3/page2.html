Hello!

My name is Andrea Lustgarten and I am from Caracas, Venezuela. I am a creative advertising student
with minors in art and interactive media at the University of Miami.
Prior to college I focused primarily on drawing and mark making to achieve
realisim in my works. However my concentration shifted from hand techniques to the digital side.
I believe the future of art is in the digital area.
My interests include eating cereal, going to the beach and sleeping.
And my role model is Rachel Greene.
